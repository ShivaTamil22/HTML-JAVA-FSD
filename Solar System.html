The Planets

Mercury

Mercury is the closest planet
of the sun. It is the smallest
planet and has very high
temperatures during the day.

Venus

Venus is the second planet
from the sun. it is called
Earth's sister planet and is
the hottest planet.

Earth

Earth is the third planet from
the Sun and the only known
planet that supports life.
About 70% of Earth is
coverd with water.

Mars

Mars is called the Red planet
because of its reddish
surface. Scientists believe
Mars may have water
in the past.

Jupiter

Jupiteris the largest planet
in the solar system. It is a
gas giant and has a famous
storm called the Great
Red Spot.

Saturn

Saturn is known for its
beautiful rings made of
ice and rock. It is the
second largest planet in
the Solar System.

Uranus

Uranus is a cold planet that
rotates on its side. It
appears blue because of
gases in its atmosphere.

Neptune

Neptune is the Farthest
planet from the Sun, It is
very cold and has
strong winds.

Other Objects in the Solar System

The Solar System also contains moond,
asteroids, comets, and dwarf planets.
while asteroids are rocky objects
found mostly between Mars and Jupiter.
